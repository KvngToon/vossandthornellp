Our Purpose

Precision.
Accountability.
Reach.

We exist because critical cargo cannot afford uncertainty. Whether you're moving electronics across three continents or routing perishables through a cold-chain corridor, every second of visibility matters.

Voss & Thorne was built by logistics professionals who were tired of systems that went dark at the border. We built the infrastructure we always needed — and opened it to the world.

Today, our platform logs over 2.4 million tracking events per day, with a 99.7% uptime SLA and a legal-grade audit trail on every shipment.

01
Chain of Custody

Every hand-off is logged. Every checkpoint timestamped. The complete provenance of your cargo is immutable and retrievable for seven years — no exceptions.

02
Network Depth

190+ countries. 2,400+ carrier integrations. Our network was built to eliminate blind spots at borders, across oceans, and in the final mile where most systems fail.

03
Regulatory Compliance

IATA, ADR, CITES, and local customs frameworks are baked into our workflows — not bolted on. Compliance documentation is generated automatically at each relevant checkpoint.

04
Client Confidentiality

As an LLP, we operate under the same duty of confidentiality as a law firm. Cargo manifests, route data, and client identities are protected by the same standards.
